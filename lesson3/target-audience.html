Target Audience

Target audience is business owners and residents in the Sandy City area.

Personas

First persona would be a business owner. The Sandy City Chamber of Commerce, or SCCC, provides many benefits to local businesses who choose to join. Membership benefits included continuing education for small businesses, networking, advertising, legislation representation, and multiple social events.

Second persona would be a resident of Sandy City. Residents can refer to the SCCC website to obtain a list of registered business, stay current on local legislation, and community events.

Scenarios

  • 1. New startup business looking for networking opportunities.
  • 2. Registered business looking for advertising opportunities.
  • 3. Registered business looking at calendar for social events.
  • 4. Resident looking for a local business to help with a need.
  • 5. Resident looking for community events on calendar.
